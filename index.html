About Us

MLD event group advertise your event or company. Experienced & Committed Support for the Event Industry.

We want to be an important part of your event and your team, effectively becoming stakeholders in your event. If your event is a success for you then it’s a success for us.

We operate transparently, with honesty and commitment far beyond expectations and contractual obligations. All of our team are time served and experienced in the event, festivals and exhibition industry's. We are here working directly for our event clients and also subcontracting to other event suppliers and companies.

We are proud members of NOEA, the AFO and Sussex Chamber of commerce, we have proudly achieved Safe contractor status and we are also working towards further industry accreditations and recognition.

Nominated for an "Arun business award" in 2022, Shortlisted for "Best Crew provider 2022" at the Event Production Awards Shortlisted for "Site Team Of The Year" at the Festival Supplier Awards 2023 Shortlisted as a finalist for "Best Events Team" 2022 at the NOEA awards All of our team are DBS checked and we are GDPR compliant and registered with the ICO.

Our in house team have been working as a team at events for over 12 years.

We pride ourselves on our qualifications and dedication as well as our commitment to the industry and our clients. As a team we are well versed in the real-world practicalities of event and festival management (Rain or shine), the regulations and guidance that we adhere and operate too.
