What We Don't Do
  • Security staffing – We are happy to recommend several very good & reliable security staffing suppliers. MLD Event Group do not provide staffing of this type. We limit our security staffing provision to qualified & experienced SIA licenced managers & supervisors only. Our managers are happy to work alongside other security providers but we effectively provide you with the knowledge & control on your event team to ensure that your security & staffing provider is correctly meeting expectations, regulations & safe systems of work.
  • We do not provide large volumes of staffing & will never provide agency staffing contracted from a third party to your event. All of our team are long standing, time served & experienced. We are a tight “family” team & we will never over commit our services.
  • Highway / traffic management – we do have SWQR qualified supervisors but we don’t provide Traffic management, lay down, planning or staffing. We are happy to recommend companies that do.
  • We don’t do rigging, Stage hire, PA systems, lighting or electrical supply but are happy to recommend some excellent companies that do.
  • We don’t supply toilets, water, plant or other event infrastructure directly, any hire that MLD Event Group under take on an events behalf is subject to our 20% profit and attendance costs.
  • We don’t do any form of Promotional work on behalf of events.
